# NPPP Protocol Roadmap

UserMint Network develops infrastructure primitives for verifiable digital evidence.

---

## Layer 1 — Evidence Notarization (NPPP v1)

Status: Launching March 26, 2026

Capabilities:

• deterministic evidence bundles  
• SHA-256 proof strings  
• replay verification  
• proof ledger entries  

Pricing model:

$0.10 per notarization  
Verification is free.

---

## Layer 2 — Evidence Registry

Future work:

• public proof explorer  
• search across notarized artifacts  
• artifact lineage tracking  
• verification dashboards  

---

## Layer 3 — Infrastructure Evidence

Future capabilities:

• CI/CD notarization  
• infrastructure state proofs  
• supply chain integrity  

---

## Layer 4 — AI Evidence

Future capabilities:

• AI model lineage proofs  
• dataset integrity proofs  
• inference audit trails  

---

## Long Term Direction

NPPP evolves toward a universal evidence protocol for:

• software systems  
• AI systems  
• compliance artifacts  
• digital records representing real-world assets

The protocol aims to make verification programmable.
